About our farms

Dina Farms belongs to Dina Agricultural Investments Company.. It is a member company of (Othman Group). Othman Ahmed Othman Group is considered one of the largest and most famous companies in the Middle East.. The history of the group dates back to the fifties of the twentieth century. Our farms were established in the eighties of the twentieth century by a prominent Egyptian figure who reclaimed and developed the desert, engineer Hussein Othman, who wanted to create a model for farms based on standard specifications for management and technological methods to contribute to the growth of the Egyptian national economy and a good destination for development and development in the future.
